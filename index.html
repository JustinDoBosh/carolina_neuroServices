Who We Are

Carolina NeuroServices - The Head Injury Center is a neuropsychological practice established in 1991 by P. Jeffrey Ewert, Ph.D., ABPP. Our mission is to help with the evaluation and rehabilitation of individuals who have cognitive and psychological impairment secondary to neurological disorders. At our location in south Charlotte, we provide neuropsychological consultations and evaluations, as well as psychosocial therapy and several treatment programs designed for individuals who have, or who suspect they may have a neurological, psychological, or cognitive impairment.
