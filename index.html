Who We Are

Carolina NeuroServices - The Head Injury Center is a neuropsychological practice that aims to improve the health and well-being of individuals who may be experiencing neurological problems as well as secondary cognitive impairments or adjustment problems secondary to a neurological disorder. Established in 1991 by P. Jeffrey Ewert, Ph.D., ABPP, our practice provides neuropsychological consultations, testing services and evaluations, as well as several types of therapy and treatment programs designed for individuals who have, or who suspect they may have a neurological, psychological, or cognitive impairment.

Carolina NueroServices

P. Jeffrey Ewert, Ph.D. ABPP

Dr. Ewert attained his doctorate degree in Clinical Neuropsychology from the University of Houston, and he now specializes in the evaluation and treatment of children and adults who have congenital and acquired brain injuries. As a member of the International Neuropsychological Society, the National Academy of Neuropsychology, and the American Psychological Association, Dr. Ewert has conducted research in respect to the effects of brain injuries. In addition to having lectured throughout the southeast and having published numerous reports on head injury rehabilitation for adults and adolescents, Dr. Ewert has testified as a head injury expert in legal cases for many years.
