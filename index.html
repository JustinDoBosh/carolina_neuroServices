Carolina NeuroServices - The Head Injury Center was established in 1991 by P. Jeffrey Ewert, Ph.D.. Dr. Ewert obtained his doctorate in Clinical Neuropsychology from the University of Houston, and now specializes in the evaluation and treatment of patients who have suffered head injuries. He is a member of the International Neuropsychological Society, the National Academy of Neuropsychology, and the American Psychological Association. Dr. Ewert has conducted research regarding the effects of brain injury on procedural memory, in addition to having lectured ____, and publishing numerous papers on head injury rehabilitation for adults and adolescents. Furthermore, Dr. Ewert testified as an expert in brain injury legal cases throughout the Southeast for many years.

At our location in South Charlotte, we provide neuropsychological consultations, testing services with evaluation, as well as therapies and treatments for individuals experiencing neurological problems resulting in cognitive impairment and adjustment problems secondary to a neurological disorder. A complete list of programs and services we offer are explained in detail on the services menu. We are the official providers of concussion management and neuropsychological evaluations for the Charlotte Checkers.
