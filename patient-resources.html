Appointment Information

If you are interested in making an appointment, please read over all of the information on this page prior to scheduling your appointment.

For an initial appointment, you will need to bring with you the following:

  • Proper photo identification (i.e. driver’s license or school ID if under the age of 16).
  • Insurance card (primary and secondary if applicable).
  • Authorization number for your visit (call the ‘mental health’ side of your insurance to obtain this number).
  • Proper co-pay or payment for the appointment, as payment is expected at time of service unless proof of coverage through your insurance is shown. We accept Visa, Mastercard, cash, or check.
  • Your completed Carolina NeuroServices

Insurance Information

Below is a comprehensive list of insurances we are in-network with:

  • Aetna Behavioral Health
  • BCBS NC (not HMO)
  • BCBS SC (only mental health side = Companion Benefit Alternatives)
  • Carolinas Healthcare System (must go through Carolina Behavioral Health Alliance)
  • Cigna Behavioral Health
  • First Health
  • MedCost (including the former Health Care Savings)
  • Magellan Behavioral health
  • Medicare
  • NC State Employees
  • Private Health Care Systems
  • Tricare For Life/Champus
  • United Behavioral Health

We are not in network with:

  • Humana
  • Coventry
  • Medicaid
  • Blue Medicare
  • Primary Physician Care

Please be sure to call the “mental health” side of your insurance prior to your first visit with Dr. Ewert for your authorization. Bring this number with you on your first visit! If you have any questions regarding your specific insurance, please feel free to call our office at 704-366-9930.
